This addon bridges **Ecliptic Seasons** with the **Let’s Do series**, ensuring crops, animals, and landscapes fully
adapt to the seasonal cycle. Experience farming and survival with greater immersion and realism.

## Features

| Mod                      | Integration Highlights                                                      |
|--------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Let’s Do: Farm & Charm   | Integrates crop growth requirements                                         |
| Let’s Do: Herbal Brews   | Integrates crop growth requirements                                         |
| Let’s Do: Vinery         | Integrates crop growth requirements and fixes snow-covered block models     |
| Let’s Do: Beachparty     | Integrates crop growth requirements                                         |
| Let’s Do: Brewery        | Integrates crop growth requirements                                         |
| Let’s Do: WilderNature   | Integrates crop growth requirements and adds seasonal animal breeding rules |
| Let’s Do: Meadow         | Adds seasonal animal breeding rules                                         |
| Let’s Do: BloomingNature | Adds snow-covered variants of grass plants                                  |
